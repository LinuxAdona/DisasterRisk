User Roles

Administrator

  • Full access to all system features
  • Manage evacuation centers
  • Register and track evacuees
  • Monitor donations and inventory
  • Approve and manage users

Volunteer

  • Register and update evacuees
  • Update evacuee statuses
  • Receive and verify donations
  • Sort and categorize donations
  • Distribute supplies to evacuees

Donor

  • Donate food and non-food items
  • Specify evacuation center for donations
  • View history of past donations
  • Get notified when donations are used
  • Limited access to sensitive data
